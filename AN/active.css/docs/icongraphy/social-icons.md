## Social Icons

Use Font AUI Icons for social icons, preferrably as a typeface. Stick to the regular or rounded styles, and avoid the circle style.

For Active products, use grayscale(ish) colors (e.g. River Bed, Rolling Stone, etc.) For marketing sites, use the brandâ€™s official colors.
